Hyderabad: Traffic restrictions near Erragadda Metro Station for 90 days

Posted on 26 March,2023//Traffic rules, Hyderabad Traffic

In view of the remodeling works of nala from AG Colony to Laxmi Complez at Erragadda Metro Station, being taken-up by Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation (GHMC) for 90-days from Tuesday, March 28 to July 28, 2023, the Hyderabad traffic police will divert traffic at several sections in the regions where the works are underway.



Uttar Pradesh: 38 school girls test Covid positive in Lakhimpur Kheri

Posted on 25 March,2023//Covid cases, Covid-19

Thirty-eight girl students of the Kasturba residential school in Mitauli block of Uttar Pradesh's Lakhimpur Kheri district have tested positive for Covid-19 infection, a district health official said. Lakhimpur Kheri Chief Medical Officer (CMO), Santosh Gupta, said that one staff member was also found Covid-positive during a contact tracing exercise on Sunday





IPL 2023: Star Sports launches subtitles feed for hearing-impaired fans

Posted on 27 March,2023//IPL2023, Sports

Star Sports, the official television broadcasters of IPL 2023, on Monday announced the launch of a Subtitled Feed, a first-of-its-kind initiative in sports broadcasting in India, for hearing impaired fans. The innovative feature will provide live match commentary subtitles, catering to the needs of differently-abled fans.
